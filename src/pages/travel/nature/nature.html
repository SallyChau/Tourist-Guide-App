Mountains & Beaches
Roque Nublo Monumento natural del Roque Nublo, 35369 Tirajana, Las Palmas

Roque Nublo is one of the highest mountains of the island. It is located in the centre of the island and attracks many visitors as it belongs to Gran Canaria's landmark.

Pico de las Nieves Pico de las Nieves, 35329 Vega de San Mateo, Las Palmas

With a height of 1949m Pico de las Nieves is the highest point of the island. Sometimes, you can even find snow here during the winter season.

Montaña Cardones en Arucas Montaña de Arucas, 35400 Arucas, Las Palmas

On top of the volcano Montaña de Arucas you can find a viewing platform which offers a fabulous view on Las Palmas de Gran Canaria.

Dunes of Maspalomas Dunes of Maspalomas, 35100 Maspalomas, Las Palmas

The Dunes of Maspalomas in the southeast of the island cover a width of two kilometers and a length of six kilometers. The dunes are a natual reserve.

Pilancones Natural Park Pilancones Natural Park, 35128 San Bartolomé de Tirajana, Las Palmas

The natural park covers an area of 5794,4 hectare and is home to many animal and plant sepcies.

Reserva Natural Especial de Guigui Reserva Natural Especial de Guigui, 35478 Tasartico, Las Palmas

Güigüi or Guguy is a special nature reserve and an area of ecological sensitivity near La Aldea de San Nicolás in the southwest of the island of Gran Canaria. It is quite difficult to reach the reserve crossing mountains. Take your time for this trip!

Playa de Las Canteras Paseo de las Canteras, Belén de arena, 35008 Las Palmas, Las Palmas

Las Canteras is a long beach close to the city of Las Palmas de Gran Canaria. It offers both great waves for surfers and nice water for snorkeling and a refreshing swim.
