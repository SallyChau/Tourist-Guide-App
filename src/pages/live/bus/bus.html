Use public transport like a local!

La Guagua

In Las Palmas de Gran Canaria, most people get around by car or by bus. Busses are also called Guagua. The two major public transport companies are Guagua and Global.

You can distinguish them based on their colors. Guagua busses are yellow while Global busses are blue. While the yellow busses only transfer within the city of Las Palmas, you can take a Global bus to reach further destinations such as the airport or Maspalomas.
