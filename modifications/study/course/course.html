Classes

You want to register or change your classes?

At the beginning the management of the courses in a new city can be overwhelming. But don't worry, there are people you can ask for help!

The first person to contact would always be the one in charge of the foreign students at your faculty. You most certainly already emailed him previous to your stay.
He will be able to help you with any questions regarding the univerity and especially your courses.

Additionally you can always contact the International Mobility Point which is located on the Tafira Campus.
