Tafira

The university consists of three campuses located all over Las Palmas which share the faculties.

The main campus is located outside Las Palmas in Tafira. It contiains the International Mobility Point,
the canteen and the university shop amongst other things are located there. Furthermore it includes...

..the Escuela de Arquitectura ..the Escuela de Ingeniería Informática ..the Escuela de Ingenierias Industriales y Civiles ..the Facultad de Ciencias de la Actividad Física y el Deporte ..the Facultad de Ciencias del Mar ..the Facultad de Ciencias Jurídicas ..the Facultad de Economia, Empresa y Turismo ..the Instituto Universitario de Microelectrónica Aplicada ..the Instituto Universitario de Sistemas Inteligentes y Aplicaciones Numéricas en Ingeniería ..the Instituto Universitario de Turismo y Desarrollo Económico Sostenible ..the Instituto Universitario para el Desarrollo Tecnológico y la Innovación en Comunicaciones

Obelisco

The university consists of three campuses located all over Las Palmas which share the faculties.

The second campus lies in the middle of the city around an obelisc. It consists of...

..the Facultad de Ciencias de la Educación ..the Facultad de Filología ..the Facultad de Geografía e Historia ..the Facultad de Traducción e Interpretación ..the Estructura Teleformación ULPGC

San Cristobal

The university consists of three campuses located all over Las Palmas which share the faculties.

The third campus is located in the south of Las Palmas with a big hospital and the Ciencias de Salud.
